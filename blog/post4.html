Sustainable Web Design

November 18, 2021

In Tom Greenwood’s article, “Sustainable Web Design, An Excerpt”, Greenwood discusses two factors of web design that cause carbon emissions and how we can create websites that reduce the amount of carbon emissions in web products. He goes into detail about how both the data transfer and carbon intensity of electricity affect the amount of carbon emissions in websites. He then explains how you can utilize them both to calculate how sustainable your web projects are.

I think manipulating the data transfer and carbon intensity of electricity factors is a very innovative and smart way of quantifying how environmentally harmful your web designs are. Before this article, I had not thought about how websites give off carbon emissions which contribute to climate change and damage the environment. I like how he combined science, coding, and design to give designers ways to be sustainable. Overall, the article gave me a new perspective on web design and how it can be utilized to help save the planet.

Lauren Hong
