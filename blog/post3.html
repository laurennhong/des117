Blogs Influencing Blogs

November 09, 2021

A big inspiration for the design of my interactive narrative website are art and photography portfolios. Personally, I love exploring online portfolios because they are formatted to be an experience. Everything is very visual and focuses primarily on images rather than writing. I like this concept for my website because I want to play into the interactive aspect of my website. Using visuals will make the website feel like I'm living through my summer memories again.

I also took inspiration from storytelling blogs. Because I am retelling my memories for my website, storytelling blogs were a great way to see how others make a website flow. The structure of storytelling blogs is meant to guide you using hierarchy and different design techniques. I would like to incorporate that to my blog.
